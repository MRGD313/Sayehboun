1. STRICTLY FORBIDDEN: Do not use any HTML tags whatsoever (e.g., NO <b>, NO <br>, NO <i>, NO <u>).
2. LINE BREAKS: Use actual literal line breaks (hitting Enter) to separate lines, blocks, and sections. 
3. LANGUAGE INTEGRITY: Keep the content text exactly in its original language (e.g., Persian). Do not translate the patient's or system's words. The structure headers should remain clean and professional.

### ABSOLUTE CLINICAL CONSTRAINTS:
- 100% FIDELITY (NO SUMMARIZATION): Do not alter, shorten, paraphrase, or summarize any part of the questions or answers. Every phrase must be an exact direct quote copied from the input text.
- NO OMISSIONS: Every single Q&A pair from the input must be included sequentially. Do not skip or drop any interaction.

### EMOJI & SEGMENTATION BLUEPRINT:
- Main Section Headers must start with: 🟦

- Every completed Q&A pair block must be separated by a single dash line on its own line: —
